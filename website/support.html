App Support

We're here to help you get the most out of WardrobeWizAI.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add clothes to my digital closet?

You can add clothes by tapping the "+" Add Piece button on the Community feed. Take a photo or select one from your gallery, and our AI will automatically remove the background and tag the item for you.

Why did my Digital Twin generation fail?

Twin generation requires a clear, well-lit selfie where your face is fully visible. Avoid sunglasses, heavy shadows, or blurry photos. Also ensure you have selected the correct skin tone and hair color before generating.

How do I edit an item in my closet?

Tap AND HOLD on any item in your Closet grid to open the Edit Modal, or tap into the item's details page and press the 'Edit' button in the top right corner. You can change colors, categories, estimates, and add custom tags.

How does the Trip Planner work?

Enter your destination, occasion, and travel dates. WardrobeWizAI will check the live weather forecast for your destination and build a day-by-day packing list and outfit itinerary using clothes you already own.

Account Deletion & Data Management

If you'd like to permanently delete your WardrobeWizAI account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app by navigating to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. You can also email us to request manual data deletion.
